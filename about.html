// the problem

learning is broken.

Every existing edtech platform builds content for the masses — one educator teaching many. Nobody adapts to the individual.

FOR LEARNERS
  • Same content, same pace for everyone
  • No real-time knowledge gap detection
  • No understanding of attention or behaviour
  • Passive learning, zero engagement
  • High effort, low retention — effort ≠ outcome
FOR ENTERPRISES
  • Fragmented, one-size-fits-all training systems
  • No infrastructure for personalisation at scale
  • Limited visibility into skill gaps
  • Manual workflows across content & evaluation
  • Security risks with external AI tools
"The bottleneck isn't content — it's understanding the learner. Information today is static. Humans are not."
